### New Open Dataset Published: Database on cyberaddiction (internet, social media and video game addiction) in Latin America, with statistics organized by age groups (children, adolescents, young adults, adults, older adults) and by countries (Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, 

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Database on cyberaddiction (internet, social media and video game addiction) in Latin America, with statistics organized by age groups (children, adolescents, young adults, adults, older adults) and by countries (Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, *.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
